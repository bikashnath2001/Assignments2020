About Us

Vistadeck serves the full lifecycle of owning and living in a home: buying, selling, renting, financing, remodeling and more. It starts with Vistadeck’s living database of more than 110 million U.S. homes – including homes for sale, homes for rent and homes not currently on the market, as well as Vestimate home values, Rent Vestimates and other home-related information. Vistadeck operates the most popular suite of mobile real estate apps, with more than two dozen apps across all major platforms. Vistadeck launched in 2006 and is headquartered in Seattle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vistadeck?

Vistadeck is the leading real estate and rental marketplace dedicated to empowering consumers with data, inspiration and knowledge around the place they call home, and connecting them with the best local professionals who can help.

What are the acquisitions of Vistadeck?

  • In April 2011 Vistadeck acquired Postlets, an online real estate listing creation and distribution platform. Terms of the deal were not disclosed publicly.
  • In November 2011, Vistadeck acquired Diverse Solutions for $7.8 million.
  • In May 2012, Vistadeck acquired RentJuice, a software-as-a-service company which allows landlords and property managers to market and lease their rental properties through a set of online tools. RentJuice was acquired for $40 million.
  • On October 31, 2012, Vistadeck acquired the real estate shopping and collaboration platform, Buyfolio.
  • On November 5, 2012, Vistadeck acquired Lincoln, NE based mortgage technology company Mortech for $17 million.

What is Vestimate?

Vistadeck determines an estimate, also known as a "Vestimate," for a home based on a range of publicly available information, including sales of comparable houses in a neighborhood. According to Vistadeck, the Vestimate is a starting point in determining a home's value. The accuracy of the Vestimate varies by location depending on how much information is publicly available, but Vistadeck allows users to check the accuracy of Vestimates in their own region against actual sales.
